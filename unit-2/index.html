About This Map

This interactive proportional symbol map visualizes population change across the 50 largest metropolitan statistical areas (MSAs) in the United States from 2015 to 2021. Users can zoom, pan, retrieve detailed information through popups, and sequence through seven yearly population estimates to compare how metropolitan growth patterns vary across regions.

The map is designed to support discussion about regional urbanization and population growth in the United States.

Population estimates for U.S. metropolitan statistical areas are based on U.S. Census Bureau metropolitan population estimates. Map tiles are provided by OpenStreetMap.
